Our inspirational slogan, "A house is a machine for living in," draws its origins from the visionary French Architect Le Corbusier. Le Corbusier was the pioneer of the concept of living in apartments or similar structures, a revolutionary idea during his time. Furthermore, his profound influence on the utilization of exposed concrete, commonly known as brutalism, has left an indelible mark on architectural history. Although not universally appreciated in modern times, some of his most iconic creations, such as Villa Savoye and the Chapel of Notre Dame du Haut, continue to captivate and inspire.

When we employ this slogan, our aim is to underscore the paramount significance of functional architecture. We firmly believe that one's home, where they spend their most cherished moments and invest their precious resources, should be meticulously designed to cater to their specific needs and desires.

Projects

FVT Architecture specializes in small to medium-scale architectural projects, prioritizing excellence in residential design and comprehensive building plans. Our expertise extends to encompass a wide range of services, including new house plans, additions, alterations, and the legalization of existing building work, including detailed as-built drawings. We are also well-versed in industrial building plans, particularly tenant installations and facilitating changes in occupation classification for existing structures.

Our portfolio boasts noteworthy achievements, such as the creation of innovative office spaces for esteemed clients like Panasonic in Ermelo, as well as the development of state-of-the-art mini factories in Maputo. These industrial and commercial ventures stand as shining examples of our ability to bring visionary concepts to life, fostering efficient and functional work environments.

At FVT Architecture, we combine our meticulous attention to detail with a deep understanding of regulatory requirements to deliver projects that exceed expectations. Whether it's residential or industrial architecture, our commitment to excellence remains unwavering, ensuring that every client's vision is transformed into a reality that enhances their daily lives and professional pursuits.

Fvt Architecture

Francois van Tonder embarked on his architectural journey in 1999, venturing into the realm of small-scale design projects as an independent consultant. Simultaneously, he assumed the esteemed role of a part-time lecturer, imparting his knowledge and expertise in Computer-Aided Design (CAD) to eager minds at the distinguished Technikon Pretoria. His passion for education extended beyond the Department of Architecture, as he dedicated five years to enlightening students in the intricate field of Horticulture at the same revered institution. Furthermore, during the period of 2012-2013, Francois served as a lecturer at the Inscape Education Group, where he adeptly transmitted the intricacies of architectural draughting and architectural technology.

With a B-Tech degree in Architectural Technology, specializing in management, Francois van Tonder stands as a beacon of proficiency and competence in his field. He now assumes the role of a Professional Senior Architectural Technologist, spearheading FVT Architecture as its sole practitioner. Possessing a profound understanding of architectural principles and a keen eye for detail, Francois skillfully navigates the complex intricacies of his craft, crafting spaces that harmoniously blend functionality and aesthetic appeal.
