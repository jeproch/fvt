Projects

FVT Architecture specializes in residential and industrial architectural projects, providing comprehensive building plans and design excellence. They offer a wide range of services, including new house plans, additions, alterations, and legalization of existing building work. FVT Architecture's portfolio showcases innovative office spaces and state-of-the-art mini factories, demonstrating their ability to bring visionary concepts to life. With meticulous attention to detail and a deep understanding of regulatory requirements, they consistently exceed client expectations, creating spaces that enhance both daily lives and professional pursuits.

About

Francois van Tonder began his architectural journey in 1999, working on small-scale design projects as an independent consultant. He also served as a part-time lecturer, teaching Computer-Aided Design (CAD) at Technikon Pretoria. In addition, he taught horticulture at the same institution for five years. From 2012 to 2013, Francois worked as a lecturer at Inscape Education Group, focusing on architectural draughting and technology.

With a B-Tech degree in Architectural Technology, specializing in management, Francois van Tonder is a highly skilled Professional Senior Architectural Technologist. He leads FVT Architecture as its sole practitioner, bringing expertise in architectural principles, attention to detail, and the ability to create spaces that seamlessly blend functionality and aesthetics.
